Linguordle

Welcome to Linguordle: The Language Genealogy Guessing Game!

Linguordle is a game that lets you test your knowledge of the genetic relationships between languages. Every time you guess, the game tells you the closest shared classification between your guess and the answer. There's a new language to guess every day, and to help you get started, the game tells you the language family of the target language.

Languages include only living, non-isolate, non-artificial languages. Some aspects of the game take inspiration from Metazooa by Trainwreck Labs.
